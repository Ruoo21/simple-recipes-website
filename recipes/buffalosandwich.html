Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

This recipe should take you only about 10 minutes to prepare, serves 6 people, and takes 6 hours to cook. Personally, I like adding blue chesse or ranch dressing, and think that these sandwiches are perfect for those days spent watching football.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • Â½ (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.
